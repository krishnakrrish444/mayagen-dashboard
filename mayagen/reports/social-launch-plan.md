## 1. Campaign Strategy: "Faster, Better, Mayagen"

### Core Concept
The migration to Next.js isn't just a technical detail; it's a massive upgrade to the user experience. We are positioning this as **Mayagen 2.0**, focusing on speed, seamless interactivity, and a frictionless creative flow.

**Tagline:** *Creativity at the Speed of Thought.*

### Key Pillars
1.  **Speed:** Instant loading, faster generation times, zero lag.
2.  **Stability:** No more crashes during complex workflows.
3.  **Future-Ready:** Laying the groundwork for upcoming "Pro" features.

### Target Audience
-   **Creators/Designers:** Care about workflow speed and tool reliability.
-   **Developers/Tech-Savvy Users:** Appreciate the tech stack upgrade (Next.js).
-   **General AI Enthusiasts:** Want the "newest" and "best" version.
